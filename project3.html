Machine Learning Based Patient Classification

This project develops a machine learning-based system to classify patients into indoor (hospital admission) or outdoor (outpatient care) categories. It uses clinical data such as vital signs, haematology parameters, and patient history to assist healthcare professionals in decision-making.

Problem Statement

Deciding between indoor and outpatient treatment is often subjective and time-consuming. Manual classification depends heavily on human judgment, which may lead to inconsistencies, delays, and misclassification especially in busy hospitals with limited experts.

Objectives

  • Develop a machine learning model for patient classification.
  • Identify key clinical features influencing classification.
  • Reduce human error and provide data-driven decision support.
  • Validate performance using metrics like accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score.
